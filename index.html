About

I'm Annette Arrigucci, a software developer currently working with SCADA software programming and testing. Outside of work I'm focused on front-end web development with AngularJS, JavaScript, HTML and CSS, but I have also worked with ASP.NET MVC, C# and Azure on the back-end. I recently started a user group for JavaScript in Augusta, Georgia, because I think it's helpful (and fun) to learn programming with other people. If you're interested please contact me for details.

Before I started working as a developer I managed content for local news websites for eight years. Motivated by some negative changes in the news industry, in late 2015 I decided that I wanted to move in a new direction. So in 2016 I attended Coder Foundry, a full-time, 12-week programming "boot camp." Coder Foundry teaches full-stack web development using the Microsoft stack. You can see several of the projects I completed at Coder Foundry in my portfolio, including a software bug tracker, car recall finder and budgeter application.

Even though my career has changed I will always treasure my experiences at three news organizations. My years in the news business opened my eyes to my community and taught me the skills necessary to run a professional website, including developing strategies to gain audience, managing a team of digital producers, managing professional social media accounts, gathering web analytics, publishing photos and videos to the web, writing articles and copy editing.

When I'm not working or coding I'm reading news or books, hanging out with my husband, on the phone with my family or planning my next travel adventure.
